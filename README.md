## 🎯 About the application

Hermes Agent Desktop helps you manage tasks on your computer. It uses artificial intelligence to help you write text, organize your notes, and simplify complex projects. This tool acts as a companion that stays on your screen to help with your work. It bridges the gap between your needs and the power of large language models. You do not need to know how to code to use this tool.

## ⚙️ System requirements

This application works on computers running Windows 10 or Windows 11. Your computer needs at least 4 GB of RAM to run the system smoothly. It also requires an active internet connection to process requests. Make sure your computer has at least 500 MB of free storage space before you begin.

## 🚀 Running the software

Once the installation finishes, you will see a new icon on your desktop. Double-click this icon to start the application. A small window appears in the bottom right corner of your screen. Click the text box inside the window to type your first prompt. Press the Enter key on your keyboard to send your message. The agent will read your request and provide a response in the chat window.

## 💡 Using artificial intelligence

This software uses advanced language models to assist you. You can ask the agent to summarize long documents or write emails. It also helps you draft creative content or edit existing work. You can change the tone of the response by clicking the settings menu. You can choose between a professional tone or a casual tone. The agent adapts to your preferences over time.

## 🛠️ Managing settings

Click the gear icon in the main window to open settings. Here you can change how the agent appears on your screen. You can move the window to any corner or keep it in the center. You can also view your recent chat history by clicking the clock icon. Use the search bar in the history section to find past requests. If you want to clear your data, click the Reset button in the privacy tab.

## 🛡️ Privacy and data

Your data stays on your local machine whenever possible. The agent sends text requests to a secure server for processing. It does not store your private documents or files. You control access to your data at all times. You can toggle the data collection switch to off if you prefer not to share usage statistics.

## 🧩 Troubleshooting common issues

If the application does not open, check your internet connection first. Sometimes a firewall might block the software. Check your antivirus settings to allow the Hermes Agent through your security wall. If the chat window stays blank, restart the application. If the problem persists, uninstall the program and download it again from the main page. This clears any errors caused by a partial installation.
